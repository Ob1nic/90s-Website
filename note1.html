THE CONTRADICTION AND DANGER BEHIND RELIGIOUS POLITICS.

By Nicolas| October 23, 2022

Certainly, conversion and persuasion are part of the political career, no
matter how far persuasion can go.So these artifices have been contradictory
and dangerous for the unfolding of social policies, and especially for humanity.
The political power uses specimens of religious gurus to disseminate its
"truths", determine what the political faithful should read, follow, talk about,
and especially attack.

The problem is the attacks on everything and everyone who does not
agree with the same ideas. Reflecting on the greatest religious
contradiction...idolatry. They really forget the importance of iconoclasm that
their religions teach them, and today idolize humans and especially
politicians.
